YouTube Audio Library

License: Free to use in videos; some tracks require attribution Login: Optional (download without login)
Best for: Background/intro music for class videos and student projects

Attribution: Some tracks: Attribution required (check track info)

Filter by mood, genre, duration; includes sound effects

Free Music Archive (FMA)

License: Creative Commons & public‑domain tracks (varies by song) Login: No login to browse; account for playlists
Best for: Wide variety by genre; discovery via tags and charts

Attribution: Often required — check each track’s CC license

Verify license on the individual track page before use

Mixkit

License: Mixkit License – free for commercial/educational use, no attribution required Login: No login
Best for: Polished stock music for intros, trailers, slideshows

Attribution: Not required (attribution appreciated)

Also offers free sound effects and video clips

Incompetech (Kevin MacLeod)

License: Creative Commons (Attribution) or paid license for no attribution Login: No login
Best for: Genre‑tagged background music; classroom-friendly catalog

Attribution: Required under CC BY

Familiar tracks widely used in education videos

Bensound

License: Free with attribution for web/YouTube; paid options for broader use Login: No login to download free MP3
Best for: Corporate/background styles; instrumental focus

Attribution: Required under free use

Check each track’s permitted uses under the free license

Jamendo

License: Creative Commons for personal projects; Jamendo Licensing for commercial Login: Optional to browse; account for favorites & downloads
Best for: Indie artists; mood‑based playlists

Attribution: Varies by track (CC BY/NC etc.)

For school/public uploads, choose tracks with appropriate CC terms

Freesound

License: CC0, CC BY, CC BY‑NC (varies per file) Login: Required to download
Best for: Sound effects & ambient loops to layer under narration

Attribution: Varies; many require attribution

Always check license on each clip; cite contributors when required

Musopen

License: Public domain recordings & scores (PD) and some CC Login: Optional to browse
Best for: Classical music for history/literature projects

Attribution: Not required for PD; recommended where possible

Great for Vivaldi, Beethoven, etc. without copyright issues

Pixabay Music

License: Pixabay License – free, no attribution required Login: Optional (download without account)
Best for: Background tracks across many genres; easy filtering

Attribution: Not required (attribution appreciated)

Also includes SFX; license prohibits compiling tracks for distribution

Upbeat

License: Free tier with attribution required; paid tier removes attribution limits Login: Required
Best for: Modern, polished tracks curated for creators

Attribution: Required on free plan

Use the provided credit link in descriptions or slides

ccMixter

License: Creative Commons (varies per track) Login: No login to browse/download
Best for: Remixes and stems; creative classroom sound design

Attribution: Usually required (CC BY/NC etc.)

Use filters for 'Free for commercial use' or 'Instrumental'

FreePD (Free Public Domain)

License: Public domain tracks (no copyright) Login: No login
Best for: PD music organized by mood; simple licensing

Attribution: Not required

Helpful for slideshows where attribution text is undesired

SoundCloud (Creative Commons filter)

License: Creative Commons (varies per track) Login: No login to browse; account for downloads (if enabled)
Best for: Community tracks; find CC BY tracks via filters

Attribution: Often required; check license per track

Verify off‑platform permissions for uploads/edits

Epidemic Sound (for awareness)

License: Subscription library (not free) Login: Required (paid)
Best for: High‑end projects; included for completeness

Attribution: Not required with active license

Use only if your school/teacher account has an active license
